STUDY TIPS

Tips for effective study skills

INTRODUCTION

When you study, you devote your time and attention to
gaining knowledge and skills in a subjest area. this often
requires disipline where you need to do researc,reading,
identification of important information and taking for the
purpose of increasing your knowledge and skills in the topic.

Effective study skills

Studying is a skill tht you can acquire through
proper disipline.Here are some practical study skills for success.

TIME MANAGEMENT

Schedule a certain time of the week devoted mainly for Studying.
Make sure thta your space for sudying is uncluttered
and free from any distractins such as social media.
Make a weekly list over the subject areas that
you need to Study.A planner or calendar is helpful.

READING AND COMPREHESION

SKIM- start by clarifying your purpose when reading
a material. This will set the tone for your reading and compreahention.
Skim the title,summary, and main
points of the chapter.The headings and sub headings will high-light the key ideas in the text.
QUESTION- You might devise questions as you
progress through your reading based from the hedings
and sub-headings. This will help you concentrate and
test as-sumptions you might have of the topic.
READ- Budget your time over how many minutes you
will devote for each chapter or section. Recite
or visualise. For some topics, these
approaches are helpful for retention and memorisation.
REVIEW- Read summary sections of the material
where available or note your own summaries - These will be useful when reviewing the section in future.
